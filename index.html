// adventure · open world · unreal engine 5

An open-world adventure game built entirely from scratch in Unreal Engine 5. Inspired by the world of One Piece and the gameplay loop of Blox Fruits — every system is being constructed from the ground up deliberately, as a way to force deep technical understanding of how games actually work.

The project is intentionally ambitious. The goal is not just to ship a game but to learn the hard parts by building them — sound design, skeletal mesh pipelines, cinematic cutscenes, VFX, and performance optimisation that keeps the game running across different hardware.
